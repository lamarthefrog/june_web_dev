A Table You Can Mess Up

This page includes a table that you can populate with random 4 digit values. The table colors will also change depending on the size of that 4 digit value. 0-2499 will be red, 2500-4999 will be orange, 5000-7499 will be yellow, and 7500+ will be green. If you don't like the numbers you get, feel free to hit the button again.
